This file contains temperature and pressure measurements related to the
SDSS imaging camera and SDSS spectroscopes.


#### Naming Convention
<code>idSysLog-[0-9]{5}\.par\.gz</code>,
where <code>[0-9]{5}</code> is the MJD.
